About Us

Founded in 2023, New Line Maritime brings together a team of seasoned professionals, including former Merchant Navy officers, united by a shared passion for excellence in maritime services. Based in the vibrant city of Belfast—a historical maritime hub renowned for its legacy of shipbuilding and innovation—we are perfectly positioned to serve the dynamic needs of the modern maritime industry. Our primary operations span the UK and Ireland, but our expertise and services extend across global waters, delivering world-class solutions wherever they are needed.
